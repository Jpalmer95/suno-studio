## Prompt Engineering Tips

### Do:
- Describe the **dynamic journey**: "starts sparse, builds to full orchestra"
- Specify **BPM**: "95 BPM" or "slow 70 BPM" or "fast 140 BPM"
- Build a **vocal persona**: "smoky baritone with grit" not just "male vocalist"
- Use **specific instruments**: "upright bass" not "bass guitar"
- Include **era/vibe**: "90s boom bap" or "retro 80s analog synths"

### Don't:
- Use artist names (Suno blocks them): say "90s grunge" not "Nirvana style"
- Keep it too vague: "sad song" → "melancholic ballad with sparse piano and intimate vocals"
- Overload with tags: 5-8 [metatags] per section max
- Contradict yourself: don't put [Calm] and [Aggressive] in same section

### Style Field Formula:
```
Genre + Mood + Era + Instruments + Vocal Style + Production Quality + Dynamics
```

Example:
```
Cinematic orchestral electronica, brooding minor key, modern production,
layered analog synths and live orchestra, male baritone vocalist with
restrained power, starts with atmospheric pads and distant percussion,
builds through verse with layered strings, chorus explodes with full
orchestra and driving electronic drums, bridge strips to solo piano,
final chorus returns with added choir
```

## Expected Results

With a good prompt, expect:
- **Generation time:** 30-90 seconds
- **Variations:** Suno typically generates 2 versions per prompt
- **Quality:** State-of-art (v4.5/v5) with proper prompts
- **Output:** MP3 audio + Suno link for each version

Pick your favorite variation, then optionally:
- Extend it (make it longer)
- Generate stems (separate vocals/instruments)
- Create variations (similar style, different take)

## Troubleshooting Bad Results

### Song doesn't match prompt
- Add more specific details to Style field
- Increase Style Influence to 70-80
- Use more [metatags] in lyrics section

### Vocals sound wrong
- Specify gender explicitly: [Male Vocals] or [Female Vocals]
- Add vocal descriptors: "smoky baritone" or "breathy soprano"
- Use phonetic respelling for unusual words in lyrics

### Structure is flat
- Add more [metatags]: [Intro], [Build-up], [Drop], [Outro]
- Describe the dynamic arc in Style field: "starts quiet, builds to explosive chorus"
- Use energy tags: [Low Energy], [High Energy], [Explosive]

### Too experimental / weird
- Lower Weirdness to 20-30
- Increase Style Influence to 70-80
- Be more specific and less abstract in Style field

### Not experimental enough
- Raise Weirdness to 60-80
- Lower Style Influence to 30-40
- Add "experimental" and "unusual" to Style field
